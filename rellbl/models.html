A simple factorized relLBL model.

Parameters:
  • rel_vocab (numpy.ndarray) – Factorized vocabulary embedding tensor for relations.
  • ent_vocab (numpy.ndarray) – Factorized vocabulary embedding tensor for entities.
  • rel_unigram_probs (numpy.ndarray) – Matrix of unigram log-probabilities for relations.
  • ent_unigram_probs (numpy.ndarray) – Matrix of unigram log-probabilities for entities.
  • rel_class_probs (numpy.ndarray) – Matrix of log-probabilities for relation classes.
  • ent_class_probs (numpy.ndarray) – Matrix of log-probabilities for entity classes.
  • embedding_dim (int) – Dimensionality of word/entity/relation/etc. embeddings.
